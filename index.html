Our Services

Keeth is a construction drawing company based in Mullathivu, Sri Lanka, providing a full range of technical drawing and blueprint services for building projects. With a commitment to excellence and a passion for creating detailed, accurate plans, Keeth has become a leading provider of construction drawing services in the region.

01. Construction

Our company specializes in building construction services. With years of experience in the industry, we offer a wide range of services from planning to completion.Trust us to deliver innovative and cost-effective building solutions.

02. CAD drawings

CAD drawings typically include floor plans, elevations, sections, and details of a building's construction, and can be used to produce construction documents and visualize the final product in 3D.

03. 3D Drawings

Our company specializes in creating stunning 3D drawings for a variety of purposes. Our team of skilled artists uses state-of-the-art software to bring your ideas to life with intricate details and vivid colors.
